Who are we?

We are a small local coffee shop located somewhere on earth! We sell a variety of coffee and baked goods. Contact us for more information!
Why our coffee is different?

We strive to bring variety to our customers and what makes us stand out from our competitors is the fact that we import our coffee from two very distinct regions of the world so we can offer different blends that can satisfy what our customer is looking for. We import coffee from the Honduras and Vietnam, both being the largest coffee exporters in their respective regions, Central America and Asia.

Our Honduran coffee blend is harvested from the western regions of the country including Copán, El Paraíso, La Paz, and Lempira.

Our Vietnamese coffee blend is harvested from the Central Highlands including Dak Lak, Gia Lai, Dak Nong, and Lam Dong.
